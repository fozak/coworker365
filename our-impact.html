Our Impact

At Your-S-A, we recognize that supporting students requires a deep understanding of the challenges they face today and the skills they will need for tomorrow. We define success by the measurable, positive impact we create—fostering youth development, empowering students to lead successful lives, and promoting a strong sense of social responsibility.

Our History and Results

The Center for Entrepreneurship, the parent organization of Your-S-A, has been at the forefront of entrepreneurial education since 2002. For more than 20 years, it has provided structured learning programs for aspiring entrepreneurs, equipping individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to create and grow successful ventures. Recognizing that effective entrepreneurship education requires a balance of practical tools and experiential learning, the Center offers programs such as FastTrac and Startup Club, designed to guide participants through the entire entrepreneurial journey. By the end of these programs, participants develop a fully researched and tested business plan, refining their entrepreneurial vision into actionable strategies.

The Center structures the entrepreneurial journey into five phases: IDEATE, POSITION, COMMIT, REFINE, and LAUNCH. From exploring ideas and understanding customers to preparing for market launch and scaling, participants gain comprehensive knowledge and practical experience to succeed in their ventures.

Since 2006, the Dynamic Entrepreneurship Classroom program has trained over 500 higher education instructors across 18 cities, resulting in more than 80 new initiatives at over 30 universities. Strategic partnerships with organizations like the Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation and the U.S. Department of State further amplify resources and impact.
